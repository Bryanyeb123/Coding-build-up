Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

How long do international transfers take?

International transfers typically take 1-3 business days, though times can vary based on the destination country and specific banking regulations.

What are the fees for sending money?

We offer transparent and competitive exchange rates with a low, flat fee for each transaction. You can view the exact fee before confirming any transfer.

Is my money safe with Quantum Pay?

Yes, your money is protected by bank-level security and advanced encryption. We use multi-factor authentication and fraud detection to keep your account secure.

Can I cancel a transfer after sending it?

Transfers can only be canceled if they have not yet been processed by our banking partners. You can check the status of your transfer in the app and cancel it if the option is available.

What currencies do you support?

We support over 150+ currencies for global transfers. Our platform automatically handles the currency exchange at competitive rates.
