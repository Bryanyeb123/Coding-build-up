How to send your first payment

Getting Started | 3 min

Sending your first payment is a simple and straightforward process. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Log in to your account dashboard.
  2. Navigate to the 'Payments' section.
  3. Enter the recipient's details, including their email or account number.
  4. Specify the amount you wish to send.
  5. Review the transaction details carefully.
  6. Confirm the payment. You may be required to enter a one-time password or use a biometric verification method.

Your payment will be processed and the recipient will be notified. You can track the status of your payment in the transaction history.

Setting up two-factor authentication

Security | 5 min

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your account. Here's how to enable it:

  1. Go to your 'Account Settings' or 'Security' page.
  2. Find the option for Two-Factor Authentication and click 'Enable'.
  3. You will be prompted to choose an authentication method (e.g., SMS, authenticator app).
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your chosen method. This typically involves scanning a QR code or entering a code.
  5. Save your backup codes in a safe place. These are crucial for regaining access if you lose your device.

Once enabled, you will need to provide both your password and a verification code from your chosen method to log in.

Understanding transaction fees

Payments | 4 min

Transaction fees can vary based on several factors. It's important to understand how they are calculated:

  • **Domestic vs. International Transfers:** International transfers often have higher fees due to currency conversion and network costs.
  • **Transfer Speed:** Expedited transfers may incur a premium fee.
  • **Payment Method:** Fees can differ depending on whether you use a bank transfer, credit card, or other payment methods.
  • **Amount Transferred:** Some fees are a percentage of the total amount, while others are a fixed rate.

You will always see a clear breakdown of all applicable fees before you confirm any transaction. This ensures you know the total cost upfront.

Recovering a forgotten password

Account | 2 min

If you've forgotten your password, you can easily reset it by following these steps:

  1. On the login page, click on the 'Forgot Password?' link.
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account.
  3. You will receive an email with a link to reset your password.
  4. Click the link in the email and follow the instructions to create a new password.
  5. Your new password must be at least 8 characters long and contain a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols.

If you don't receive the email, please check your spam folder or contact support for assistance.

International transfer limits

Payments | 6 min

There are limits on international transfers to comply with financial regulations and ensure security. Here are the key things to know:

  • **Daily and Monthly Limits:** You will have specific limits on how much you can transfer per day and per month. These limits are displayed in your account dashboard.
  • **Currency Conversion:** Limits are often based on the converted currency value.
  • **Verification Level:** Higher verification levels or different account tiers may have increased transfer limits.

If you need to transfer an amount exceeding your current limit, please contact our support team. We can assist with increasing your limits after a quick verification process.
